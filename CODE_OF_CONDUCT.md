# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone. Be respectful, give actionable technical feedback, protect private
information, and assume good faith while holding changes to documented quality
and security standards.

Project maintainers may remove content or participation that is abusive,
threatening, discriminatory, deceptive, or disruptive. Report conduct concerns
privately to the repository owner. Enforcement decisions are made by Wayne Tech
Lab LLC and may include warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
