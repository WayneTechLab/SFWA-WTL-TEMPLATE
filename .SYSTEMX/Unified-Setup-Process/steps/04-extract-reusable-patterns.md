# Step 04 — Extract Reusable Patterns

## Goal
Convert repo observations into reusable template patterns.

## Actions
- Identify patterns for setup, deploy, CI, MCP, security, docs, and runbooks.
- Exclude app-specific business logic, branding, data, customer flows, and
  private operational details.
- Add findings to setup notes or module docs.

## Gate
Reusable patterns are documented without copying product-specific code.
